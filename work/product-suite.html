Overview

Led the UX strategy and design for a comprehensive platform consolidation—unifying multiple legacy products into a modern, AI-enhanced SaaS solution. This initiative required synthesizing disparate user experiences, extracting value from existing systems, and establishing a scalable design foundation that could evolve with emerging AI capabilities while serving both legacy and new user bases.

The Challenge

Transform multiple legacy products into a unified SaaS platform while integrating AI capabilities. This required balancing the familiar with the new—extracting value from existing systems while building a scalable foundation that could evolve beyond legacy constraints.

Key Challenges

  • Legacy consolidation: Reconciling disparate UX patterns and workflows from multiple existing products
  • Data limitations: Minimal unified user research across legacy platforms
  • Evolving target market: Redefining personas as we unified previously separate user bases
  • AI integration: Designing for emerging AI capabilities while maintaining core functionality
  • Technical debt vs. innovation: Determining what to preserve, what to rebuild, and what to reimagine
  • Scalable architecture: Building a flexible system that supports both current needs and future AI-driven features
  • Change management: Helping existing users transition while attracting new ones

Solution

We developed a unified design system that bridges legacy patterns with modern capabilities—creating a flexible foundation that serves existing users while enabling AI-enhanced features. The modular architecture allows us to evolve specific components without disrupting core workflows, supporting both immediate consolidation needs and long-term innovation.

Scalable information architecture and user flow mapping
Foundational design system and pattern library
Flexible architecture supporting future product expansion

Design Process

Our process balanced synthesis of existing knowledge with new discovery—extracting insights from legacy products while validating assumptions through rapid prototyping and testing. This approach allowed us to honor what worked while confidently modernizing outdated patterns.

1

Legacy & Market Analysis

Audited existing products to identify successful patterns and pain points. Conducted competitive analysis and stakeholder interviews to understand market gaps and define our unified product vision.

2

User Research & Consolidation

Synthesized fragmented user data across legacy platforms and conducted new research to redefine personas for our unified user base. Created journey maps that bridged existing workflows with new capabilities.

3

Design System & Architecture

Built a modular design system that harmonizes disparate legacy patterns while establishing modern standards. Designed scalable information architecture that accommodates both current features and future AI integration

4

Prototyping & AI Integration

Created interactive prototypes testing core consolidated workflows and emerging AI features. Validated concepts with both legacy users (testing transition) and new users (testing modern experience).

5

Iterative Testing & Refinement

Conducted continuous user testing throughout development with both existing and target users. Iterated based on feedback, usage analytics, and AI feature performance to refine the unified experience.

Results & Impact

Successfully launched the unified platform, consolidating 2 legacy products with 3 greenfield capabilities into a single SaaS solution. Our phased AI integration strategy—starting with transparent inline actions and evolving toward copilot and recommendation experiences—is building user trust progressively, with the start of users engaging with foundational AI features. Early feedback validates our design approach and continues to inform our iteration roadmap as we scale toward more advanced capabilities.
