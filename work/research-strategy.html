Overview

Demonstrating the strategic value of user research by selecting the right methodology for the challenge at hand. When stakeholders questioned the timeline and ROI of user testing, I pivoted from slow moderated sessions to rapid unmoderated testing—validating a new progressive disclosure pattern in 48 hours and proving that research delivers actionable insights quickly when properly scoped.

The Challenge

Research Perception Problem

Stakeholders viewed user research as time-consuming and questioned its value for iterative design decisions, creating pressure to skip validation and ship unproven patterns.

Pattern Validation Need

A new progressive disclosure approach needed validation before implementation, but traditional moderated testing timelines (weeks) conflicted with development schedules.

Key Challenges

  • Overcoming perception that research slows down product development
  • Validating a new interaction pattern within aggressive timelines
  • Demonstrating research ROI to skeptical stakeholders
  • Selecting appropriate methodology that balanced rigor with speed
  • Proving quick research can deliver reliable, actionable insights

Solution

When initial moderated testing proved too slow, I pivoted to unmoderated remote testing—optimizing for speed without sacrificing insight quality. This strategic shift delivered validated results in 48 hours, proved the pattern's effectiveness with quantitative data, and demonstrated that well-scoped research accelerates rather than delays decision-making.
